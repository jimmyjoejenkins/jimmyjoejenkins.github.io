Paint in Perfect Proportion

Project any reference image directly onto your canvas or wall at any size, opacity, and depth. Your art stays in proportion.

Work Inside a Master Studio

Step into an immersive art studio, ancient ruins, or your environment of choice — and paint in your real world through a passthrough porthole.

Match Your Colors Perfectly

Built-in color sampler matches your reference to Sherwin-Williams, Winsor & Newton, Liquitex, and Lanyani Ceramic Mosaic palettes.

Developed by Jimmy Joe Jenkins

Jimmy Joe Jenkins is a Fort Worth, Texas muralist with 52 years of experience painting public art, commissioned murals, and large-scale works. He built MuralEye because no tool like it existed — so he created it himself.
