Murals with MuralEye

Divine Drift

A solitary figure floats weightlessly through an endless current, surrounded by Monarch butterflies as symbols of transformation and spiritual guidance. Gold accents illuminate beauty that could not exist without change.

Divine Drift — In Perspective

The full corridor view of Divine Drift, showing the immersive scale of the installation. Painted using MuralEye to maintain perfect proportion across the entire wall.

Friday Night Legacy — Keller Sports Park

Football has long been woven into the identity of Keller. This mural captures determination and intensity while the iconic Keller "K" stands as a symbol of community pride.

Western Spirit — Keller Sports Park

A tribute to Keller's ranching roots and the independent spirit of the American West. The rodeo queen symbolizes confidence, tradition, and community celebration.

Forward Together — Keller Sports Park

Soccer is a game of movement, vision, and teamwork. This mural captures the focus and determination that defines every great athlete — and the community that surrounds them.

Every Great Season Begins Here — Keller Sports Park

Inspired by vintage Americana, this mural honors the countless young athletes who dream big every time they step into the batter's box.

Texas Royalty — Five Iron Golf

A Longhorn rests upon a vintage sofa — ranch icon reimagined as an unexpected portrait of quiet confidence. Vivid color symbolizes the evolving identity of modern Texas.

The 19th Hole — Five Iron Golf

Downtown Fort Worth reimagined through bold contemporary color. The city skyline, classic automobiles, western heritage, and modern culture collide into a single vivid celebration.

Rocker B Ranch — Corrugated Tin

One of the most challenging surfaces in mural work — no straight lines, no flat plane. Ten murals painted on rusted corrugated tin in perfect proportion. The field test that proved MuralEye, May 2026.
