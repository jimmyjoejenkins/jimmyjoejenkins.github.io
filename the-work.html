Murals with MuralEye

Divine Drift

A solitary figure floats weightlessly through an endless current, surrounded by Monarch butterflies as symbols of transformation and spiritual guidance. Gold accents illuminate beauty that could not exist without change.

Divine Drift — In Perspective

The full corridor view of Divine Drift, showing the immersive scale of the installation. Painted using MuralEye to maintain perfect proportion across the entire wall.

Friday Night Legacy — Keller Sports Park

Football has long been woven into the identity of Keller. This mural captures determination and intensity while the iconic Keller K stands as a symbol of community pride.

Western Spirit — Keller Sports Park

A tribute to Keller's ranching roots and the independent spirit of the American West. The rodeo queen symbolizes confidence, tradition, and community celebration.

Forward Together — Keller Sports Park

Soccer is a game of movement, vision, and teamwork. This mural captures the focus and determination that defines every great athlete — and the community that surrounds them.

Every Great Season Begins Here — Keller Sports Park

Inspired by vintage Americana, this mural honors the countless young athletes who dream big every time they step into the batter's box.

Texas Royalty — Five Iron Golf

A Longhorn rests upon a vintage sofa — ranch icon reimagined as an unexpected portrait of quiet confidence. Vivid color symbolizes the evolving identity of modern Texas.

The 19th Hole — Five Iron Golf

Downtown Fort Worth reimagined through bold contemporary color. The city skyline, classic automobiles, western heritage, and modern culture collide into a single vivid celebration.

Rocker B Ranch — Corrugated Tin

One of the most challenging surfaces in mural work — no straight lines, no flat plane. Ten murals painted on rusted corrugated tin in perfect proportion. The field test that proved MuralEye, May 2026.

Murals

Wild Harmony — Poppies & Pollinators

Nature's smallest inhabitants often have the greatest impact. This mural celebrates the delicate relationship between hummingbirds, pollinators, and native wildflowers, reminding us that beauty and survival exist through balance.

The Best Maid Story — A Texas Original

Few brands have become as woven into Texas culture as Best Maid Pickles. This mural honors nearly a century of local history, craftsmanship, and hometown pride.

Wings Over the Trinity — Blue Heron

The Great Blue Heron has long been a quiet guardian of the Trinity River. Captured in mid-flight, the bird symbolizes patience, resilience, and the importance of preserving the natural spaces that define Fort Worth.

Transformation — Butterfly on the Hill

This sculptural butterfly explores transformation in both form and meaning. One wing is rendered as solid color and presence, while the other dissolves into an open framework.

The Giving Tree — Cherry Blossom

Created as a place for reflection. Centered beneath its flowering canopy hangs a simple swing, inviting visitors to pause, remember, and reconnect.

Crystal Springs Hideaway — A Toast to Texas Tradition

Inspired by classic advertising illustrations and vintage Americana, this water tower transforms a familiar landmark into a celebration of Texas hospitality.

Rocker B Ranch — The Land Remembers

This mural honors the enduring relationship between ranchers, livestock, and the Texas landscape. Open skies, grazing cattle, and distant horizons capture the quiet strength of ranch life.

Independence Day — When the Crows Finally Leave

Inspired by the symbolism of crows, the work reflects the intrusive voices of fear, addiction, anxiety, and self-doubt that often circle endlessly overhead. Created on July 4th.

Fort Worth Garage — The Mechanics of Memory

Before automobiles became collectibles, they were part of everyday life. This mural celebrates the craftsmen, mechanics, and working families who helped build the city one repair at a time.

Goodnight Hall — The Spirit of the West

Created for Rocker B Ranch, this piece pays tribute to the cattle drives, ranching traditions, and pioneering spirit that shaped Texas.

Cowtown Mosaic — A Modern Tribute to Fort Worth

This large-scale mosaic celebrates the identity of Fort Worth through one of its most recognizable symbols — the Texas Longhorn.

Peony & Dragonfly — Moments of Stillness

This mural magnifies a quiet moment in nature, transforming an ordinary flower into an immersive experience.

Four Seasons of Poppies — A Journey Through Time

Stretching across multiple corridors, this immersive mural transforms architecture into a continuous landscape as poppies bloom through changing skies and shifting light.

Rick's White Light Diner — Louisiana After Dark

Inspired by the Louisiana bayou, this mural embraces folklore, humor, and Southern storytelling — playful alligators, frogs, cypress trees, and glowing evening skies.

The White Light Gentleman — A Quiet Observer

This mural captures the timeless elegance of a solitary figure, quietly observing the world around him. A small bird resting on his hat introduces an unexpected moment of life.

Seasons of Renewal

Created for a flood-control wall, this mural transforms ordinary infrastructure into a celebration of changing seasons — cherry blossoms, fresh leaves, butterflies, and native plants.

Three Wise Monkeys — Perspective

Inspired by the familiar proverb, this mural reimagines the concept through humor and contemporary symbolism.

Velvet Box — Timeless Elegance I

Inspired by the glamour of mid-century Hollywood, this mural celebrates confidence, beauty, and classic femininity.

Velvet Box — Timeless Elegance II

Companion to the first mural, this portrait continues the celebration of classic glamour through expressive realism.
